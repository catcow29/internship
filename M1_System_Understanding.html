03   Key Findings
Healthcare Platform Analysis
  • DoctorKeeper is a SaaS-based platform integrating patient records, diagnostic images, and prescriptions
  • DICOM-format X-ray, MRI, CT images stored in the cloud and linked to AI diagnostic modules
  • Data access managed via Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with HIPAA compliance structure
Medical AI Dataset Research
  • NIH ChestX-ray14: 112,120 chest X-ray images labeled with 14 disease categories
  • ISIC Skin Lesion Dataset: benchmark dataset for skin lesion classification tasks
  • Class imbalance identified as a core challenge in medical AI model training
Basic Image AI Concepts
  • CNN (Convolutional Neural Network): core architecture for image feature extraction
  • Overview of major classification models: ResNet, VGG, EfficientNet
  • Transfer Learning concept and its application cases in the medical imaging domain
04   Deliverable
Submitted 3–5 page analysis report covering platform structure, dataset overview, and basic image AI concepts
05   Insights & Reflection
Learned that medical AI requires strong focus on explainability and regulatory compliance beyond simple accuracy. In real-world clinical settings, the cost of False Negatives (missed diagnoses) is extremely high, making Recall a critical evaluation metric alongside accuracy.
